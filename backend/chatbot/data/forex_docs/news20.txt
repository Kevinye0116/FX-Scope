# New Zealand Myfxbook NZDUSD Sentiment

### 
The Myfxbook NZDUSD Sentiment Indicator is a very important tool for traders. This indicator shows the percentages of buyers and sellers of NZDUSD. It helps you make better decisions by looking at the most recent market patterns and identifying where the market is likely to change direction. It is based on Live accounts connected to Myfxbook.
